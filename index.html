Privacy Policy

Espresso Shot Log

Effective date:

Espresso Shot Log is a minimalist espresso shot log designed to help you track and improve your espresso workflow. This Privacy Policy explains what data the app collects and how it is used.

Data collected

Espresso Shot Log collects only the following data types:

  • Name — used to label or personalize your espresso logs.
  • Photos — optional media you choose to add (for example, latte art photos).

How your data is used

The data you provide is used solely to display and organize your espresso logs within the app. It is not used for advertising, analytics, or marketing.

Data storage

All data is stored locally on your device. Espresso Shot Log does not require an account and does not upload your data to external servers.

Data sharing

Your data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

Data retention and deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.

Children's privacy

Espresso Shot Log is not directed to children under the age of 13, and no data is knowingly collected from children.

Changes to this policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page by updating the effective date.
